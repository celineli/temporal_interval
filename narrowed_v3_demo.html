Cond 1: classic Michotte launching — clean left ball slides in, contacts right ball, right ball slides away after the delay.   Cond 4: right ball (agent) has a face, eyes toward the approaching ball, stays still, expression flips happy→sad after the delay.   Color-change control: identical setup to Cond 1, but the right ball never moves — instead it changes color after the delay. This isolates whether tolerance for delay in Cond 4 is driven by the mental/social nature of the effect, or simply by the right ball remaining stationary.
